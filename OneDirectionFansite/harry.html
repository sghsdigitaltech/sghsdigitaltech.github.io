Harry's Life

Harry was born in Worcestershire, the home of the famous british sauce. He spent his early life going to school like regular people until he won a 'Battle of the Bands' competition and got his big break.
Since becoming a rockstar Harry has spent half his time in London and half his time in California. He likes to spend his free time making the word a better place and playing music.
