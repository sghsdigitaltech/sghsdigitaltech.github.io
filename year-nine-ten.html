SGHS DIGI TECH IN YEAR 9/10

Computer Bytes

In computer bytes students learn about designing and developing digital outcomes using common computer software. They learn to create a range of different outcomes - for instance digital print in Microsoft Word, vector images with Adobe Illustrator, and basic websites with Google Sites.

Digital skills are an increasingly important part of all jobs and the goal of these course is to equip students with the skills they might need regardless of their exact direction.

Code Busters

In codebusters students learn about how digital devices work and how to create new digital programs. Students will be introduced to text-based computer programming and how to use HTML/CSS to construct web pages. It will also dive deeper into computer science topics and cutting-edge technology.

This course teaches students skills useful for a digital focused career. This includes traditional digital careers such as programmers and web developers, but also areas of increasing digital usage such as science and design.
