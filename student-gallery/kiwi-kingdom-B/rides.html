Rides & Activities

Haunted House

The Haunted House is a spooky roller coaster featuring twists, turns, and sudden drops. The entire ride is in pitch black so you never know what’s coming next. Perfect for 12+ who like thrilling rides.

Merry Go Round

Ride a fake horse around in circles for 5 minutes. What a fun activity! The horse moves slowly up and down, and there’s carnival music playing in the background. Perfect for 4+ or with a parent.

Kevin's Kitchen

In this entertaining show our mascot Kevin the Kiwi will teach you how to cook kiwi food like pavlova, cheese rolls, and fish and chips. Yum! It’s educational and entertaining. Perfect for all ages.
