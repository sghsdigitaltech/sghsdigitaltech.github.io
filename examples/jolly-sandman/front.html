About the Jolly Sandman

The Jolly Sandman hotel was founded in 1893 by Louise Sanderson. The hotel has undergone many historic changes since then, including the addition of indoor plumbing and air-conditioning.

Our Rooms

All of our rooms come with our famous "Bed Bug Free Gurantee" - if you find any bed bugs in your room, we will give you a $10 meal voucher for our in house restaurant. Within your room you'll find beds made from genuine wood, and blankets made of genuine wool.
