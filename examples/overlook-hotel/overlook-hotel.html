The Overlook Hotel

The best hotel you will ever visit

Nestled in the Rocky Mountains in scenic Colorado, our hotel has been a holiday spot since the early 1900s when it was a resort destination for people suffering from Tubercolosis. Nowadays you don't need to be sick to come here, in fact given the current pandemic we'd prefer it if you weren't sick.

Our hotel features many amazing amenities like bathrooms, beds and closets. Even in the coldest winter you'll stay warm thanks to our new boiler, and fear not our restaurant has lots of delicious food.
