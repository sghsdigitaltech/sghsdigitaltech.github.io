CAMP HALF LAKE

Welcome to Camp Half Lake

IMG

Camp Half Lake is the premier destination for all your holiday needs! Ever since 1947 this lovely lakeside spot has hosted families and people of all ages to relax in the surrounds of beautiful nature. We have luxury cabins for those who like their creature comforts and tent spaces for those who like to rough it up more.

IMG

As well as our fantastic accommodation we also have a range of amazing leisure activities. There's 18 holes of mini-golf, kayaking on the lake, the world's fourth largest trampoline, outdoor cinema on Tuesday nights, scavenger hunts each week, and our beautiful husky-german shepherd cross Potsy who is always up for a game of fetch.
